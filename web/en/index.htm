We are a circumcision clinic located in Hong Kong. Our qualified surgeons carry out circumcisions on all age groups of males.


We employ numerous methods to ensure the correct procedure is used. Our patient confidentiality is of the strictest practise and confidentiality is assured to the highest level.


Circumcision is the removal of some or all of the foreskin (prepuce) from the penis.





It is important that practices providing minor surgery operate to the highest possible standards.


Use disposable sterile instruments


Have approved sterilisation procedures that reflect national guidelines





We offers a relaxing environment for your child and the family.


We provide refreshments for our visitors.


We have a fully equipped theatre including a DVD entertainment system for both adults and children.




All our Doctors are specially trained in best practice to circumcise men of all ages - from infants to adults. Our doctors use the latest techniques to ensure the least possible trauma.
